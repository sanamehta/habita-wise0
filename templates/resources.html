Frequently Asked Questions

What are my basic rights as a tenant?

As a tenant, you have the right to a habitable living space, privacy, security deposit protection, and freedom from discrimination.

How do I handle maintenance issues?

Document the issue, notify your landlord in writing, and keep records of all communications and repairs.

What should I do before signing a lease?

Read the entire lease, document existing damage, understand your responsibilities, and get all promises in writing.

How can I protect my security deposit?

Document the condition of the property at move-in and move-out, follow lease terms, and provide proper notice before moving.
